Wordclarify

Use your smartphone to define words in paper books.

Wordclarify is an OCR-based dictionary app. Improve and expand your English vocabulary with ease while reading any paper book.

How it works:

Make sure you have sufficient lighting while scanning words. Mobile OCR is best suited for black text on a flat white background.

  1. Aim the frame at the word you want to define.
  2. Tap anywhere once on the screen to improve focus.
  3. Press the trusty camera button to snap a quick picture of the word.
